I am Archisman (Archis). I am a coder, entrepreneur and Product Manager. I work at PixFirst. PixFirst is an e-commerce platform for professional and amateur photographers. We help photographers serve their customers better and increase their sales. If you are a photographer in France, you should definitely check us out.

When am not working on PixFirst, I spend my time reading (a lot) and sometimes writing (a bit) in English, python and few other languages. This website started as an exercise to improve my severely handicapped html/css skills.

A bit about my past life

Prior to PixFirst, I was a founder at Edumata Technologies where we built an Online Admission Management platform for schools.

My first job was as a Product Analyst at Trilogy. I was responsible for launching Zozoc in India and later South-East Asia and development of a new J2ME client. Zozoc was text messaging mobile application that worked over GPRS/2G. (yes, a bit like whatsapp) At its peak, we had over 5 Million users worldwide.

I am an alum of Trilogy University 2007. Harvard Business Review did a case study on it in 2001. You can find it here. In their own words, it was "No ordinary boot camp".

I graduated from IIT, Kharagpur in 2007 with a major in Computer Science and Engineering.
